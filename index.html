🚨 KEY SAFETY REMINDERS

🚫

Food Allergies

No nuts or shellfish allowed. Some young people are dairy and egg intolerant.

🚗

Parking

There is no car park, but plenty of parking on the roads nearby.

🏃

Public Access

Members of the public, including dog walkers, may pass through the area. This could cause concern or discomfort for some young people.

Area Boundary

Teamwork

🕒 SESSION TIMELINE

8:00am

Staff member to pick up first aid kit and forest school equipment from centre.

8:30am

All staff to meet at the bottom of the forest school road and help bring all forest school equipment up to the meeting point.

8:45am

Staff to set up forest school equipment ready.

9:35am

Staff to discuss who's coming, any concerns, the plan, and roles for the meetup.

9:50am

Two staff members go down and meet the young people at the bottom.

10:00am

Young people arrive and walk up with staff. Member-led activities are done throughout the session, keeping it fun and relaxed.

11:45am

Session starts to wind down, and staff go down and wait with the young people at the bottom for their parents.

12:00pm – 1:00pm

Staff have lunch and discuss teens session, who's coming, any concerns, the plan, and roles for the meetup.

12:50pm

Two staff members go down and meet the young people at the bottom.

1:00pm

Young people arrive and walk up with staff. Member-led activities are done throughout the session, keeping it fun and relaxed.

2:45pm

Session starts to wind down, and staff go down and wait with the young people at the bottom for their parents.

3:00pm

Staff members tidy up the area and pack up all forest school equipment, take equipment down to the car. Staff return forest school equipment back to centre.

🌲 AREA SETUP

Fire Area
Make sure fire area is clear and tidy. Lisa will set up fire area.
Trees
Hammocks to be secured between suitable trees. Zipline to be fixed to appropriate anchor trees. Multi-function rope (with attachment points for swings/seats/climbing equipment) to be installed between trees and tensioned safely. Also install two parallel ropes (upper and lower) between trees for additional activity routes.
Main Swing
Set up safely the main swing on the big tree using the weighted throwing rope to get the rope swing over the tree.
Boundary
Make sure the grounds within the boundary are clean and tidy and that staff know what is within the boundary.
